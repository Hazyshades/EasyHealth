## 1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website or use an online service.

Cookies help websites and apps remember information about your visit, such as login status, preferences, language settings, security state, and usage activity.

We may also use similar technologies, such as local storage, session storage, pixels, tags, SDKs, and device identifiers.

## 2. Why we use cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

1. Keep you signed in.
2. Secure your account.
3. Remember your preferences.
4. Provide core app functionality.
5. Understand how the service is used.
6. Improve performance and reliability.
7. Personalize your experience, where permitted.
8. Measure marketing campaigns, where permitted.

## 3. Types of cookies we use

### Essential cookies

Essential cookies are required for the service to work properly.

They may be used for authentication, session management, fraud prevention, security, load balancing, consent storage, and core app functionality.

You cannot disable essential cookies through our cookie banner because the service may not work without them.

### Analytics cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how users interact with the service.

They may collect information such as pages visited, features used, errors, session duration, device type, browser type, approximate location, and performance metrics.

We use analytics to improve the product, fix bugs, and understand which features are useful.

Analytics cookies are optional where required by law.

### Personalization cookies

Personalization cookies help us remember your preferences and tailor the service to your needs.

They may be used to remember language, display preferences, recent activity, saved settings, and content preferences.

Personalization cookies are optional where required by law.

### Marketing cookies

Marketing cookies may be used to measure campaigns, understand referral sources, and show relevant content.

We do not use marketing cookies to sell health data.

Marketing cookies are optional and will only be used where permitted by law and your preferences.

## 4. Cookies and health data
